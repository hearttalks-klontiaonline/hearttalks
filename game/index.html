*In the EMPEROR-Reduced® trial, a randomised, double-blind, parallel-group, placebo-controlled study of 3730 adult patients with HFrEF (NYHA class II, III, or IV and LVEF ≤ 40%), the efficacy and safety of JARDIANCE 10 mg (n=1863) was evaluated vs placebo (n=1867) in addition to standard of care. The primary composite endpoint in the EMPEROR-Reduced® trial was a composite of CV death or HHF, analysed as time to the first event.
